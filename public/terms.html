What this is

The Teen Money & Momentum Map is an AI-guided, self-reported developmental snapshot about money, direction, and follow-through. It is not therapy, mental-health treatment, a clinical or diagnostic assessment, financial advice, or a crisis service.

Who it's for

Set up by a parent/guardian for a teen aged 13–17. Ages 18+ are directed to a separate Young Adult experience. It is intended for use in the United States.

AI disclosure

The conversation and result are generated by an AI system. Results are interpretive, not definitive, and should be read as a starting point for a conversation — not a verdict.

No guarantees

We provide the Map "as is." It may be unavailable at times and may contain errors. It is not a substitute for professional advice.
