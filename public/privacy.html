Outsmart the System — Teen Money & Momentum Map

Privacy

Plain-language summary. This is a draft pending legal review.

What we collect

To set up the Map, a parent/guardian gives their own first name and email, the teen's first name, and the teen's age. During the Map, the teen's typed answers are processed to produce their result and a draft report.

What the teen controls

The teen sees their result first. Nothing personal reaches the parent unless the teen previews it line by line and approves it. If the teen keeps it private, nothing is sent and the draft and transcript are deleted.

How long we keep it

A session and its data expire and are deleted within 30 days. A finished result that is never shared or kept-private is deleted sooner (within about 7 days). Anyone can delete everything immediately using the "delete my result" control, or by contacting us.

Who can see it

The teen's raw answers are not shown to the parent. We do not sell data. Answers are processed by our AI provider to generate the result. If the conversation suggests the teen may be unsafe, a designated OTS responder may be alerted — that alert contains no quotes from the teen (see Safety).

Contact / delete request

Email jay@outsmartthesystem.org to ask what we hold or to delete it.
